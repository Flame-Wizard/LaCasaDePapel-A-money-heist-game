 La Casa de Papel: Heist Arena

## Game Description
**La Casa de Papel: Heist Arena** is a 2D retro-style shooter inspired by the Netflix series "Money Heist". Players control a character attempting a high-risk bank heist, requiring fast reflexes, accurate shooting, and tactical movement to survive extreme encounters with security forces. 

The primary objective is to successfully execute the heist by eliminating threats and locating the escape route.

## Core Features & Mechanics
* **Omnidirectional Movement:** The player character can move both horizontally and vertically across the map.
* **Dynamic Health Points (HP):** The player's HP decreases when hit by enemy bullets. Conversely, HP increases when the player successfully eliminates opponents. The game ends if HP drops to zero.
* **Randomized Enemy AI:** Police units attack from random locations, requiring continuous tactical repositioning. Enemy formations are randomized to maintain high gameplay difficulty.
* **Escape & Progression:** To advance levels, players must survive until an "Escape Door" appears at a random location on the screen and enter it before being defeated.
* **Cognitive Challenges:** The game integrates puzzle-based mechanics to test and improve logical thinking alongside fast-twitch combat.

2.  Eliminate enemies to regenerate your Health Points (HP).
3.  Survive until the Escape Door randomly spawns.
4.  Reach the door to progress; the number and variety of enemies will scale up in higher levels.
